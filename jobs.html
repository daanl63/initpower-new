Lead Developer Power Platform and Azure

We have built a SaaS solution called ModelVerse that supports customers in the Netherlands and abroad in managing risks related to cyber security, OT security, physical security, patient safety, and sustainability.

Due to rapid growth, we are looking for a Lead Developer Power Platform and Azure (0.8 - 1.0 FTE).

 

What will you do?

  • Full-stack application development on Microsoft Power Platform and Azure
  • Automate business processes using Power Automate
  • Build and maintain APIs and interfaces with other SaaS services
  • Create AI applications using Microsoft and third-party services
  • Develop interactive dashboards and reports with Power BI

What are we looking for?

  • HBO degree in ICT or business informatics
  • Knowledge of Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, Dataverse, SharePoint
  • Understanding of Microsoft Azure and AI services
  • Ambition to grow and take on new challenges
  • Flexible, communicative, and proactive team player
  • Problem-solving mindset and eagerness to learn
  • Experience with Power Platform and Azure is a plus
  • Good command of English

Additional Requirements

  • Affinity with information security
  • Certificate of Conduct (VOG)
  • Living in the Netherlands with a valid work permit

What do we offer?

  • Dynamic, informal work atmosphere in a fast-growing start-up
  • Opportunity to grow into team lead of developers
  • Possibility to take on security consultancy and IT integration projects
  • Salary: €3,500 – €4,500 per month (based on 40 hours and experience)
  • 8% holiday pay and 25 vacation days
  • Flexible working hours and 2 remote days per week
  • Office at the Hague Security Delta (HSD) campus
  • Laptop if required
